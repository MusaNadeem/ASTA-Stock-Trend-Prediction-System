Chart Reading Guide

Teal Line — Close Price

The historical close price series for the selected stock over the last 60 sliding-window timesteps.

Yellow Dots — ASTA Focus

Points the ASTA model assigned highest attention weight to. These are the timesteps that most influenced the prediction.

Dashed Line — Forecast

Projected price trajectory from Transformer recursive forecasting. Run a prediction to see the AI forecast curve extend the chart.

Blue Area — Volatility

Rolling variance of the price window scaled for overlay display. Peaks indicate regime uncertainty periods.
